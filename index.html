EST. LOUISIANA LAGNIAPPE SYRUPS · BITTERS · DRY FRUIT packed with a little extra

lagniappe / ‘lan-yap / noun

A small gift given with a purchase; a bonus or extra, offered by way of good measure. A Louisiana custom — and the only way we know how to pack a box.

What we make

Cocktail Syrups

Bitters

Our story

Twenty years behind the bar, now behind the label

Lagniappe Provisions was built by a bar professional with twenty years in New Orleans service — the kind of career that teaches you exactly which syrup falls flat, which bitters actually earn their place on the rail, and which garnish is worth the extra ninety seconds.

We’re a majority woman-owned company making the products we always wished someone else would make well: small-batch, bar-tested, and honest about what’s in the bottle. No shortcuts, no filler, no fine print — just the provisions we’d stock behind our own bar.

  • Bar-tested. Every recipe is proven on a real rail, not just in a kitchen.
  • Majority woman-owned. Built and led by women in the New Orleans bar industry.
  • Always lagniappe. Every order ships with a little something extra. That’s the whole point.
